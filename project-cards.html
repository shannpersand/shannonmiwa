Standard American Playing Cards

A two year side project journey that stretched my illustration, lettering, and type design skills. It also allowed me to practice system design in a non-web format.

The project began as a redesign exercise to create a more diverse selection of face cards in a Euro-centric standard playing card deck. From this, I expanded my redesign to include the numerical card pip patterns, the backs of the cards, joker, and even the box and sticker packaging.

I printed 3,000 decks and donated the bulk of them, but they are also available for purchase.
